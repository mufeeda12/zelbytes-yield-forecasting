## Observations

* Correlation analysis was performed on temperature, humidity, CO₂ concentration, and mushroom yield.
* Scatter plots were used to visually inspect relationships between environmental conditions and yield.
* Humidity, temperature, and CO₂ concentration may influence mushroom production and should be considered as predictive features.
* Scatter plots help identify nonlinear relationships that may not be visible through correlation coefficients alone.
* The visual analysis supports the use of environmental sensor readings for yield forecasting.

## Conclusion

Exploratory Data Analysis revealed observable relationships between sensor variables and mushroom yield. These findings justify the use of temperature, humidity, and CO₂ concentration as input features for machine learning models developed in later stages of the project.
